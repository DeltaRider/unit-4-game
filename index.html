This game is meant for people 18 and older!

Deep in the heart of San Francisco's Tenderloin Distict lives the

dookémon

in their natural habitat, also known as

dookéland

. In dookéland, dookémon can be observed at all hours of the night participating in their own micro economy. This micro economy sees cash flow from normal society into dookéland, through dookéland, and eventually back out to corporate America. It's one big game where the Crackhead collects money from city dwellers (non-dookémon) passing through or parking in dookéland. The Drug Dealer sells drugs to, and collects money from the Crackhead. The Drug Dealer uses that money to pay for the Hoes. The Hoes give their money to the Pimp, and the Pimp eventually gets the Crackhead's dooké on his clothes. This forces the Pimp to buy new clothes from corporate America outside of dookéland, thus completing the full lifecycle of money flowing through dookéland's micro economy. This is the game of dookémon...

Rules of the Game

- You will choose 1 out of the 4 dookémon as your fighting character. You have a choice between the Crackhead, Drug Dealer, Hoe, or Pimp. You can evaluate each dookémon's playing card before making your choice.

- The dookémon you choose will take on the other 3 dookémon in an attempt to be the last one standing with money.

- Choose the order in which you take on the other dookémon wisely. Each dookémon has a special economic relationship with the other. No two relationships are the same.

- Press the spacebar to begin. Good Luck!
